Frequently Asked Questions

How long does dry cleaning take?

Most garments are ready within 3-4 days depending on the fabric type and treatment required.

Do you provide home pickup and delivery?

Yes. We offer convenient pickup and delivery services across Avadi and nearby locations.

Can you remove tough oil stains?

We use specialized stain-removal techniques for oil, grease, food, and other difficult stains. Results depend on stain age and fabric condition.

Do you clean silk sarees?

Yes. We provide premium silk saree cleaning with fabric-safe handling and finishing.

Can bridal lehengas be dry cleaned?

Absolutely. We specialize in cleaning bridal wear, designer wear, and heavily embroidered garments.

Do you clean blankets, carpets, and curtains?

Yes. We clean blankets, comforters, carpets, curtains, and other household fabrics.

What if a stain cannot be completely removed?

Some old or chemically bonded stains may not be fully removable, but we always use the safest treatment and achieve the best possible result.

Are garments inspected before cleaning?

Yes. Every garment undergoes inspection before processing to ensure proper care.
